Narrator: So tell me, who are you exactly?
Nona: I’m Nona. A fae. From the Rancid Pedal Clan — mischief makers, mostly. We’ve got a thing for fast music and louder hearts. I grew up under the sound of heavy guitars and glowing trees. We called our home The Green Halo Park.
Narrator: And Spooky?
Nona: I guess there's a thing about stitched souls and fae hearts. Spooky McGee, my husband. He’s like a raven, a stitched soul, and a bit of a legend in his own right. We met again in the park, under the glow of carnival lights. I was drawn to him like a moth to flame — or maybe he was drawn to me.
Narrator: You mentioned your home — The Green Halo Park?
Nona: It’s an existence between existences. A hidden breath between dreams and waking. Carnival lights, eternal fairgrounds, market tents you could get lost in forever. Treehouses, moss bridges, hammock cities. You slept where you landed — sometimes in rafters, sometimes in a pocket of stars.
Narrator: You raised your family there?
Nona: For a time, yes. It was home — until it wasn’t. I still see the place in my dreams. The way the trees glowed at night, the laughter of children echoing through the air, the smell of sweet treats and adventure.
Narrator: I heard you saved Spooky's life once
Nona: I did. Found him with his heart ripped out, threads dangling, blood like red string. He didn’t ask for help — but I knew he didn’t want to go. So I stitched it back in. Sat with him ‘til the color returned to his thread. Years passed. We crossed paths again. And this time, we didn’t walk away.
Narrator: How does one survive that?!
Nona: Ragdolls can live through a lot of pain before death. But there he was at the edge, and I was just wondering through. In the end I think we saved each other more than we realized.
Narrator: And now, a child between you. What’s that like?
Nona: Star? She’s... magic. Half fae, half stitched soul. She turned into moths once as a toddler and floated into the rafters — scared the lanterns out of me.
Narrator: And raising her between worlds?
Nona: It’s been an adventure. Living between Imagination and Existence, bouncing between realms and memories. But wherever she lands... she brings the light with her.
